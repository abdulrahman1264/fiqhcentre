FOUNDATIONS OF ISLAMIC SCHOLARSHIP

Usul al-Fiqh & Usul al-Hadith

The two pillars that govern how Islamic rulings and authentic narrations are established

Usul al-Fiqh — أصول الفقه

The Roots of Islamic Jurisprudence · علم أصول الفقه

The science defining how Islamic legal rulings are derived from scripture. It gives scholars the tools to extract rulings for any new situation — grounded in the Quran and Sunnah.

Usul al-Hadith — أصول الحديث

Science of Hadith Authentication · علم مصطلح الحديث

The science used to evaluate narrations attributed to the Prophet ﷺ — protecting the Sunnah from fabrication, weak transmission, and unreliable narrators.
